How it works

FFT pipeline: high-pass → noise floor (adaptive median over time) → SNR gate → spectral contrast check → slew rate filter.

Adaptive noise floor: tracks slow-varying noise across the spectrum so peaks stand out from constant background sources.

Spectral contrast: peak must be sharper than its neighbours, not just louder than the average. Rejects broadband noise like wind/rain.

Logging: sessions are saved to your phone (IndexedDB). Export as CSV via Share Sheet.
