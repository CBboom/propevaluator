How it works

RPM via mic: FFT finds the dominant frequency in engine's expected band. Firing rate = (RPM/60) × cyl / (stroke/2).

SNR filter: Peak must be at least N dB above the band's median (noise floor) to count.

Best 2-sec / 20s: Highest 2-second average speed within the last 20 seconds (rolling).

Slip: Theoretical (kn) = (RPM ÷ gear) × pitch ÷ 1215.2
