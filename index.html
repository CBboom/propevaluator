How it works

FFT pipeline: high-pass → adaptive noise floor → SNR gate → spectral contrast → slew rate filter.

Logging: sessions are saved to your phone (IndexedDB). View charts inline and export as CSV via Share Sheet.

Advance ratio J: J = V / (n × D) where V = boat speed (m/s), n = prop revs/sec, D = prop diameter (m).

Acceleration: smoothed GPS-speed derivative over ~1 sec window.
