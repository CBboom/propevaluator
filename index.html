How it works

RPM via mic: The app samples audio at ~22kHz and uses an FFT to find the dominant frequency in the engine's expected band. Firing rate = (RPM/60) × cylinders / (stroke/2).

Slew filter: Rejects RPM jumps above your Max ΔRPM/s threshold — physically impossible spikes (e.g. wave slap, harmonic flip).

Slip: Theoretical speed = (RPM ÷ gear ratio) × pitch ÷ 1056 [knots]. Slip % = (1 − actual/theoretical) × 100.

Note: Hold phone in a quiet-ish spot near the engine. Wind noise will hurt accuracy.
