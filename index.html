How it works

Auto-recovery: If GPS stops sending updates, the app restarts the watch automatically. Status pill turns yellow when stale.

Persistence: All settings, theme choice, and speedometer toggle are saved between sessions.

FFT pipeline: high-pass (30 Hz) → adaptive noise floor → SNR gate → spectral contrast → slew rate filter.
